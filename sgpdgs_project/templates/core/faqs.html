Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the SGPDGS Portal

General Questions

SGPDGS (Smart Gram Panchayat Digital Governance System) is a comprehensive digital platform designed to modernize and streamline the operations of Gram Panchayats. It provides citizens with easy access to government services, complaint filing, certificate applications, and official notices.

Any citizen residing in the jurisdiction of the Gram Panchayat can register and use this portal. Government officials and administrators also have access to manage services and respond to citizen requests.

Yes, the SGPDGS portal is available 24/7 for citizens to access services, file complaints, and check the status of their applications. However, processing of requests happens during office hours (Mon-Fri, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM).

Account & Registration

Click on the "Register" button on the homepage, fill in your details including name, email, phone number, and address. After submitting, you'll receive a confirmation and can log in immediately.

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, please contact support at info@sgpdgs.gov.in.

Yes, after logging in, go to your Profile page where you can update your personal information, contact details, and address. Make sure to save changes after updating.

Complaints

Log in to your account, navigate to the Complaints section, click "File New Complaint", select the category, provide a detailed description, and submit. You'll receive a complaint ID for tracking.

Go to the Complaints section in your dashboard. You'll see all your complaints with their current status (Submitted, In Progress, Resolved, or Rejected). Click on any complaint to view detailed information and updates.

Resolution time varies depending on the nature and complexity of the complaint. Typically, complaints are acknowledged within 2-3 business days and resolved within 7-15 business days. You'll receive notifications at each stage.

Certificates

You can apply for various certificates including Income Certificate, Caste Certificate, Residence Certificate, Birth Certificate, Death Certificate, and other certificates issued by the Gram Panchayat.

Required documents vary by certificate type. Generally, you'll need proof of identity (Aadhaar, Voter ID), proof of residence, and specific documents related to the certificate you're applying for. Check the certificate application page for detailed requirements.

Once your certificate is approved, go to the Certificates section in your dashboard. Find the approved certificate and click the "Download" button to get a PDF copy with digital signature.

Technical Support

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the problem persists, contact technical support at info@sgpdgs.gov.in.

The portal works best on the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
